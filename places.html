Places to visit:

Italy is basically one giant museum under the sky. Here are some must-visit places:

  • Rome:
    The heart of the Roman Empire. You can visit the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Vatican City â€” home of the Pope and the famous Sistine Chapel painted by Michelangelo.

  • Venice:
    A magical city built on water. Instead of streets, there are canals! You can take a romantic gondola ride, see the Rialto Bridge, and enjoy the Carnival of Venice with masks and costumes.

  • Florence:
    Known as the birthplace of the Renaissance, it's filled with art by Leonardo da Vinci, Botticelli, and Michelangelo. The Duomo (cathedral) has one of the most beautiful domes ever built.

  • Milan:
    Fashion capital of the world. You'll find luxury shops, modern art galleries, and delicious restaurants.

  • Amalfi Coast:
    A dreamy seaside area with colorful houses and crystal-clear water.
