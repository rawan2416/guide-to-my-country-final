Culture and Tradditions:

Italians are known for being friendly, expressive, and family-oriented.
Family is at the center of Italian life — Sunday lunches with grandparents are a must!
Fashion is part of daily culture — Italians always dress stylishly, even just to buy bread.
Festivals are colorful and full of energy — like Venice Carnival, Siena Horse Race, and Christmas markets.
Italians greet with kisses on both cheeks and talk with their hands — it's part of their charm.
The Italian language itself sounds musical — it flows beautifully and is often called “the language of love.”
