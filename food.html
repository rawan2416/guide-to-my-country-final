Popular Food:

Italian food is known and loved everywhere — it's fresh, simple, and full of flavor.

  • Pizza:
    Originally from Naples, pizza started as a street food for workers, but now it's one of the most eaten dishes on Earth. The classic one is Margherita, with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and basil -- the colors of the italian flag .
  • Pasta:
    Italy has more than 350 types of pasta! Each region has its own favorite — like spaghetti in Rome, lasagna in Bologna, and penne in Sicily
  • Gelato:
    Italian ice cream is creamier and denser than normal ice cream. People say once you try gelato in Italy, you can never go back.
  • Coffee:
    Italians take coffee seriously — especially espresso. Most drink it while standing at the counter before going to work.

Food in Italy is not just a meal — it's a moment to gather family and friends, talk, and enjoy life.
