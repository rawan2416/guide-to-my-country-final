Music and Artists:

Italy has a long musical tradition that goes back centuries.
In classical music, Italy gave us opera, a dramatic and emotional form of singing. Famous opera singers like Luciano Pavarotti and Andrea Bocelli are loved all around the world.
In modern music, Italy also has pop and romantic songs that people dance to in summer festivals.
Italian instruments like the violin and piano were both invented or developed in Italy!
